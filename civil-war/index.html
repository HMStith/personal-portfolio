Total Warfare

Sherman's march was based around Total Warfare to bring fear to the people in Georgia. Sherman gave his troops nothing to live off of except for tents to live in. Sherman's troops would steal food from people in the south for sustinance. The soldiers would burn down the barns of anyone who resisted the raid as a sort of payback. Total warfare also had Sherman's troops burn down and destroy anything of value to the confederates. This included factories, railroads, weapon storages, etc.

Southern Reaction

This section will be split into two. One for the southern generals and one for the southern civilian reactions.

Southern Generals

During the march General John Bell of the Confederate army was planning a raid to take back Chattanooga, Tennessee. John Bell hope that him doing this would force Sherman to turn around and come fight him, however Sherman was anticipating the raid and sent General George H. Thomas to deal with John Bell in Chattanooga. One the attack was over Georgia was cleared of Confederate troops and Sherman was free to continue to the ocean.

Southern Civilians

When the march reached Atlanta, Sherman's troops put it under siege. Many citizens fled south to avoid Sherman and his troops. One account from a citizen of Atlanta said, "Since Atlanta, I have felt as if ... we are going to be wiped off of the Earth." [1] Many of the people who fled south went to refugee camps, one of which was in Nashville and held thousands homeless refugees. Many anonymous civilians, such as Jennie Wade, were found dead later. This was preventable as when Sherman entered Atlanta he warned the mayor to evacutate the city, he did not do this as he claimed that the city did nothing wrong. Sherman continued with the raid as he saw war as cruety enough to justify the burning.

Modern Connections

One connection can be seen in both world wars. In both of the world wars there was submarie warfare. In submarine warfare they would bomb civilian trade ships to strike fear into the countires populas just as Sherman's troops would burn down cities to strike fear into the south's populace. The German's also bombed London so much as a show of force that they started to ship children off to the country side. Also during the post World War II era during the Vietnam War a sort of total warfare was shown by North Vietnam.
